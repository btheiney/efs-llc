WHO WE ARE. Educational Furniture Solutions, LLC was founded in 1998 and quickly gained a reputation for Exceeding Client Expectations. The sole purpose of EFS is to provide unparalleled products and service to the Education Marketplace. EFS has worked extensively on thousands of projects ranging in size from one classroom to entire buildings. We partner with our customers from Design to Delivery, to guide them through the phases of a project.
Reputable Manufacturers Representing a mix of reputable manufacturers, EFS prides itself on product knowledge. Unlike typical commercial furniture dealers who represent hundreds of manufacturers, we have chosen to be loyal to a small number and know their products intimately.
Satisfaction Guaranteed From project inception to completed punch list, EFS customer service will stand out. Customer Service is our number one priority. We value the relationships we have with our customers and will do everything to maintain them.
